About We Were Yesterday

We Were Yesterday is a DIY project from Aaron VanDuynhoven. A name used to release various solo recordings and projects. Spawning out of around 15 years of home demoing, recording, and experimentation, a process that continues. The first release, "Old South", came in 2014, and was an instrumental EP bridging post-rock and progressive styles. It was followed by "After The Rain" the following year, which was a live-in-the-room acoustic EP of songs written over the previous decade. That contrast between the heavily produced and the more raw and untouched sets the tone for future experimentation, encapsuling seemingly incompatible passions.
